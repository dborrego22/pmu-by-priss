Policy

Cancellation, restrictions, and health requirements

Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

We require proper notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. The following policy applies to all appointments:

  • 30+ Days Notice: Full refund of deposit
  • 14-29 Days Notice: 50% of deposit retained, 50% refunded
  • Less Than 14 Days Notice: Deposit forfeited (non-refundable)
  • No-Show: Full appointment fee due, no refund
  • Same-Day Cancellation: Full appointment fee due

Health Requirements & Restrictions

To ensure your safety and the best results, you must meet the following health requirements. You will not be able to proceed with your appointment if you do not meet these requirements.

Age Requirement

  • Must be 18+ years of age (valid ID required at appointment)

Medical Conditions & Medications

You are not eligible for permanent makeup if you have any of the following:

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding
  • History of keloids or abnormal scarring
  • Active skin conditions (acne, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, dermatitis)
  • Autoimmune disorders
  • Bleeding disorders or taking blood thinners (Warfarin, Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Vitamin E)
  • Recent Botox, fillers, or laser treatments (minimum 2 weeks waiting period)
  • Sunburn or recent sun exposure to face
  • Current or recent chemotherapy
  • Uncontrolled diabetes
  • Skin sensitivity to pigments or metals
  • Recent facial surgery or procedures
  • Contagious skin conditions
  • Herpes simplex virus (cold sores) — must be managed before appointment

Medications That May Affect Treatment

The following medications may interfere with the procedure or healing. Please disclose all medications at your consultation:

  • Blood thinners (aspirin, ibuprofen, warfarin, etc.)
  • Retinoids (Accutane, tretinoin, adapalene)
  • Immunosuppressants
  • Antibiotics
  • Vitamin supplements (especially Vitamin E)
  • Herbal supplements (St. John's Wort, Ginkgo Biloba, etc.)

Pre-Appointment Care

To achieve the best results and ensure a safe procedure, please follow these guidelines before your appointment:

  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning beds for 7 days before appointment
  • Do not use retinoids for 1 week prior
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before appointment
  • Do not take blood thinners (unless prescribed) for at least 24 hours before
  • Get adequate sleep the night before your appointment
  • Arrive with a clean face, free of makeup
  • Avoid caffeine on the morning of your appointment (may increase sensitivity)

Post-Appointment Care

Proper aftercare is essential for optimal results and healing. You will receive detailed aftercare instructions at your appointment. General guidelines include:

  • Keep the area clean and dry for 10 days
  • Avoid water, sweat, and humidity for the first 7-10 days
  • No swimming, saunas, or steam rooms for 10 days
  • Avoid direct sun exposure for 4 weeks
  • Do not pick, scratch, or peel the treated area
  • Avoid makeup on the treated area for 10 days
  • Do not sleep on your face for the first week
  • Avoid blood thinners and alcohol during healing
  • Do not engage in strenuous exercise for 7 days

Touch-Up & Maintenance

  • Complimentary Touch-Up: Included within 6 weeks of initial appointment (free color/shape adjustments)
  • Annual Touch-Up: Recommended annually to maintain color and shape
  • Additional Touch-Ups: Available at reduced rate after complimentary period
  • Longevity: Permanent makeup typically lasts 1-3 years depending on skin type, sun exposure, and lifestyle

By booking an appointment with PMU by Priss, you confirm that:

  • You meet all health requirements listed above
  • You have disclosed all medical conditions, medications, and allergies
  • You understand the risks and benefits of permanent makeup
  • You have read and agree to this policy
  • You understand the cancellation policy and associated fees

Failure to disclose health information may result in cancellation of your appointment without refund and potential liability for any adverse effects.
